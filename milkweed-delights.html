Milkweed Delights

Designed by Mariam Aleila, Falé Do, Sharon Ku, and Leanne Suen Fa

In collaboration with three other talented designers, I designed a sustainable cookbook that serves as an artifact to encourage the cultivation of common milkweed. The book shared information about the plant's cultural and historical importance, four milkweed recipes, and steps on planting milkweed responsibly. Our aim was to strenghten the relationship between milkweed and people by promoting milkweed as a food source.
